He recently graduated from Lewis University of Romeoville, Illinois with Bachelor of Science in Computer Science paralleled with a minor in Business Administration. Hannon stresses his time between contract work for a private company in Downers Grove and independent consulting by word of mouth.

Hannon has completed entry level courses such as Programming and Algorithm Design on through advanced courses such as Communications and Networking. Following his interest in Business, he has also taken part in such courses as Principles of Management on through Supervisory Management. Hannon shows an ultimate interest in Server/Network Administration and Desktop Maintenance in a Small Business environment.
